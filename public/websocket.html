Websocket.

Weber has WebSockets support and you can handle websocket connection and incoming/outcoming websocket message in your controllers. First of all you need to designate websocket controller in your config.ex file in webserver: section, like:

ws: true,
ws_port: 8800,
ws_mod: :Simplechat.Main.Chat
